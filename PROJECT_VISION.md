# Path of the Researcher

## Philosophy

This is not a task tracker.

This is not Duolingo.

This is not Notion.

This is a Knowledge RPG.

Learning is gameplay.

---

## Design Pillars

1. Exploration

Player explores unknown knowledge.

Not completes checklists.

---

2. Mastery

Topics are conquered.

Not checked.

---

3. Competition

Learning together should be more motivating than learning alone.

---

4. Discovery

Bosses are hidden.

Map is partially covered by fog.

Future regions are unknown.

---

5. Beauty

Everything should feel like an indie RPG.

The map is always the central screen.

---

6. AI is the Game Master

AI never owns the game logic.

It enriches the world.

Game must work without AI.

---

7. Data Driven

Every campaign is described as data.

No hardcoded ML concepts.

The engine knows nothing about Optimization or Transformers.

Only Regions, Topics, Quests and Bosses.

---

8. Expandability

Everything should be extensible.

Every mechanic should be pluggable.
